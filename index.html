关于英歌宝About Engo Baby

把世界带回普宁的英语启蒙World-class English, rooted in Puning

英歌宝是一家专注 3–6 岁幼儿英语启蒙与全英文 STEAM 创新课程的儿童教育机构。我们的创立，源于一群普宁学子留学归乡后的共同愿景——把更自然、更沉浸、更符合儿童成长节奏的母语习得体系带回家乡。Engo Baby focuses on English enlightenment and all-English STEAM for children aged 3–6. We were founded by Puning students who studied abroad and returned home, determined to bring a more natural, immersive, child-paced language approach back to our hometown.

我们相信，县级市的孩子同样值得拥有高质量的国际化语言教育资源。We believe children in a county-level city deserve world-class international language education just as much as anyone.

1
不怕英语，愿意开口Unafraid to speak

把英语还原成声音、游戏、故事与探索，先喜欢，再理解，再表达。English as sound, play, story and discovery — love it first, then understand, then express.

2
更国际化的学习方式A global way to learn

全英文课堂、主题学习、绘本、律动、TPR 与项目探索。All-English classes, themes, picture books, songs, TPR and project exploration.

3
培养双语国际人才Future bilingual talent

敢表达、会思考、能合作，用英语理解世界、表达观点。Confident, thoughtful, collaborative — understanding the world and voicing ideas in English.
